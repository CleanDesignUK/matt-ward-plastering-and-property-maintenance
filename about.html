Matt Ward Plastering & Property Maintenance

For over 30 years, we have proudly served Anglesey, Gwynedd, and the surrounding areas from our base in beautiful Beaumaris. Our team specialises in expert plastering, painting & decorating, tiling, and comprehensive property maintenance solutions. We combine decades of experience with modern techniques to transform properties into comfortable, vibrant, and durable spaces.

Whether you need a fresh coat of paint, precise tiling, or a full-scale renovation, we are committed to delivering exceptional results. Our emphasis on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction sets us apart—and keeps our clients coming back.
